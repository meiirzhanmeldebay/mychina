  • What is the procedure? When can I apply?

  • What is the procedure? When can I apply?

    You can apply now. Here is the procedure of application:
    (1)Scan and send us all required documents, after 1-2 weeks we confirm 100% whether you can get a scholarship, and it's conditions (Note: all the scanned documents should be in a good quality, (in pdf, jpeg or doc form) ,the size of the document should be no more than 1 MB.
    (2) If you like the conditions, we start application process;
    (3) Pay Application Fee, signing the Agreement;
    (4) Nearly, in June-July (depends on university), we will get your Admission Letter and JW 202 Form, then you have to pay our service charges. After, we will send you Admission Letter and JW202 Form and you can start your Visa Application process.
    (5) Come for registration at the exact time and enjoy your university and further studying ☺

  • Required documents

  • Required documents

    1. Passport
    2. 2 Photos (3x4)
    3. Visa page of current entry (if available)
    4. Current entry stamp (if available)
    5. Highest Degree Diploma (in English, notarized)
    6. Transcripts (in English, notarized)
    7. HSK/IELTS (if available)
    8. Motivational Letter (800-1000 words, Bachelor Only)
    9. Research Plan (800-1000 words, CSC ,OBOR, Jasmine( Master and PhD only))
    10. 2 Recommendation Letters (CSC ,OBOR, Jasmine( Master and PhD only))
    11. Provisional Consent Form (NJUST only)
    12. Medical Form (Ask me to send you)

  • Scholarship requirements?

  • Scholarship requirements?

    1. CSC (China Scholarship Council- Government Scholarship) • You have to meet age requirements a) Undergraduates, who are not older than 28 y.o b) Masters, who are not older than 30-35 y.o (depends on university) c) PhDs, who are not older than 40- 45 (depends on university) • Science: IELTS 6.0 and above Liberal Arts and Business: IELTS 6.5 and above ( Applicants from Native Speaking Countries need to show the certificate that he/she received previous education in English. • Science : HSK 4 (210+) Liberal Arts and Business: HSK 5 (180+) Masters and PhDs - HSK 5 for Nanjing Normal University • GPA> 3.5 (out of 4) GPA> 4.5 (out of 5) • Average grade: > 85% (No C and D)

    2. Jasmine and NMG Scholarships • You have to meet age requirements d) Undergraduates, who are not older than 28 y.o e) Masters, who are not older than 30-35 y.o (depends on university) f) PhDs, who are not older than 40- 45 (depends on university) • Science: IELTS 5.5 and above Liberal Arts and Business: IELTS 6.0 and above ( Applicants from Native Speaking Countries need to show the certificate that he/she received previous education in English. • Science : HSK 4 (180+) Liberal Arts and Business: HSK 5 (180+) Masters and PhDs - HSK 5 for Nanjing Normal University • GPA> 3.2 (out of 4) GPA> 4 (out of 5) • Average grade: > 80% (No C and D)
