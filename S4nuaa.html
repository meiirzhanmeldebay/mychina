Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics

Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics was founded in October 1952. In 1996, NUAA passed the evaluation process of Project 211 directed by the Ministry of Education, and became one of the nation’s “100 key universities for the 21st Century.” Approved by the Ministry of Education, NUAA launched postgraduate programs in 2000. In 2011, NUAA became the key construction University of “985 Project Advantage Discipline Innovation Platform". At present, NUAA has a full-time enrollment of over 8141 postgraduate students, 17924 undergraduates, and 673 international students. There are also 5873 other types of registered students in various programs. Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics attaches great importance to international cooperation and exchanges. It has established cooperation with more than 100 famous universities, research centers and enterprises all over the world. About 500 international students from 30 countries, e.g. France, Germany, Italy, Japan, Korea, Pakistan, Nepal, India, Malaysia, Philippine, Iraq, Maldives, Sri Lanka, Mauritius, Zimbabwe, Kenya, etc. are studying for doctoral, master and bachelor degrees. In2009, it has been appointed by Ministry of Education of the People’s Republic of China to accept international students with Chinese Government Scholarship.
